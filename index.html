World of Happiness

Introduction

This visualization will give viewers an overview of the interesting, livable and happiness of all countries around the world. The datasets and ranking we have is between the period 2015 - 2019. The score of happiness in each country will be calculated by combine the score of 6 different aspects : GDP score, Social support, Healthy life expectency, Freedom to make life choices, Perceptions of corruption and Generosity of people. Hope that my visualization will help viewers answer questions they expect, like 'How is the country he is living in on the ranking of the most livable countries in the world?'

Distribution of Happiness

Te visualization shows us the distribution of happiness score in all countries from the world in 2019. We can hover over the space of the country to see what is their actual score of happiness.

Relationship between others attributes score with the happiness score

This visualization shows us the relaitionship between different other's score with with score of happiness. The users can choose which score to be shown in the selection menu. Also the year slider will let people choose the data in which year will be shown between 2015-2019.

Average Happiness score in continent level

This vusalization shows us different score in coninent levels. The boxplot also show the 75% quantile with the mean in each continent of happiness score. And the bar chart will shows us the mean in all the continents.

Top 10 countries in different score

This visualisation will shows the top 10 countries which leads in each score from 2015-2019. The users can choose which score to be shown in the selection menu. Also the year slider will let people choose the data in which year will be shown between 2015-2019.
